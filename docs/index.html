Spatial Transcriptomics Analysis

Cluster: Cortex_1

Markers: Mef2c, Satb1, Nrsn1, Stx1a, 1110008P14Rik, Snap25, Serpini1, Dclk1, Dkkl1, Cabp1

This cluster likely represents excitatory pyramidal neurons of the cerebral cortex (neocortex), specifically within the glutamatergic layers. This identification is justified by the expression of key cortical transcription factors Mef2c and *Satb1, which are essential for the development and identity of cortical projection neurons, alongside mature synaptic markers like Snap25 and *Stx1a.

Cluster: Cortex_2

Markers: Nptxr, Hpcal1, Lmo3, Syn2, Slc30a3, Lypd1, Nov, Cpne6, Olfm1, Gabra2

This cluster likely represents glutamatergic pyramidal neurons of the isocortex, specifically localized to the superficial to middle layers (Layers 2/3 or 5). This is justified by the presence of Slc30a3 (ZnT3) and Nptxr, which are classic markers for telencephalic excitatory neurons, alongside layer-enriched markers such as Hpcal1, Cpne6, and Lmo3 that are characteristic of cortical pyramidal populations.

Cluster: Cortex_3

Markers: 3110035E14Rik, Tbr1, Ncald, Ttc9b, Pde1a, Hs3st2, Ipcef1, Igsf21, Tmem132a, B3galt2

This cluster likely represents Layer 6 (L6) of the cerebral cortex. The prediction is primarily justified by the presence of Tbr1, a canonical transcription factor for Layer 6 excitatory neurons, and Pde1a, which is highly specific to this deep cortical layer. Other markers like Hs3st2 and Ipcef1 further confirm a deep-layer glutamatergic identity consistent with the L6 anatomical profile.

Cluster: Cortex_4

Markers: Lamp5, Atp1a1, Rasgrf2, Igfbp6, Mef2c, Cabp1, Itpka, Calb1, Gucy1a1, Cacng3

This cluster likely represents Layer 2/3 (L2/3) pyramidal neurons of the cerebral cortex. The prediction is supported by the expression of canonical superficial layer markers such as Lamp5, Rasgrf2, Itpka, and Calb1, while the presence of Mef2c and Cacng3 confirms an excitatory glutamatergic neuronal identity.

Cluster: Cortex_5

Markers: Camk2n1, Camk2a, Slc1a2, Tspan7, mt-Nd5, mt-Atp8, Calm1, Ddn, mt-Nd2, Hpcal4

This cluster likely represents excitatory pyramidal neurons within the cerebral cortex, specifically localized to the superficial layers (Layer 2/3). This prediction is justified by the high expression of Camk2a and Ddn, which are canonical markers for glutamatergic neurons and synaptic plasticity, alongside Hpcal4, a specific marker used to delineate superficial cortical layers and the hippocampal CA1 region.
